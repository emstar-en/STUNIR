Requirements resolution

Targets -> required contracts mapping:
  contracts/target_requirements.json

Resolve requirements:
  python3 tools/resolve_requirements.py --targets wasm,c --out receipts/requirements.json

Probe required deps:
  STUNIR_OUTPUT_TARGETS=wasm,c scripts/ensure_deps.sh

Verify requirements satisfied:
  scripts/verify_deps.sh

Attestations:
  python3 tools/inspect_attestation.py <files...> --out receipts/attestations.json
